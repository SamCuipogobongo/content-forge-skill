# GZH Article Style Rules (auto-updated)
> Last updated: 2026-03-05 | Version: 1

## DO
- Title should add information value, no clickbait
- State article value in the first 3 lines ("after reading you'll know...")
- Use subheadings, 200-400 chars per section
- Weave in personal experience or industry cases for credibility
- End with engagement prompt (question, poll, comments)
- Bold key sentences as visual anchors

## DON'T
- No more than 3000 chars (unless deep-dive long-form)
- No empty openers like "as we all know", "it goes without saying"
- No pure information relay — include original viewpoints
- No overly long sentences — maintain rhythm
- No excessive self-promotion within the article
- No filler phrases like "let's take a look together"
