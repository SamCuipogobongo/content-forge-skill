# XHS Note Style Rules (auto-updated)
> Last updated: 2026-03-05 | Version: 1

## DO
- Title with emoji + eye-catching short phrase (under 15 chars)
- Clear paragraph breaks, 2-3 lines each
- Use native XHS language: "tested", "pitfall", "recommendation"
- Use emoji as paragraph markers sparingly
- End with 3-5 relevant hashtags
- Content should feel "informative": steps, checklists, comparisons

## DON'T
- No more than 800 characters (XHS users have limited patience)
- No formal/academic writing style
- No "firstly...secondly...finally" three-part structure
- No excessive hype — keep it authentic with personal experience
- No "in conclusion", "to summarize" closing cliches
- No jargon overload — write plainly
